📖 Glossary
Middle 50%
The SAT/ACT/GPA range for the middle half of admitted students. 25% scored below, 25% above. Be inside this range for a realistic shot.
COA — Cost of Attendance
Sticker price: tuition + fees + room + board. What the school advertises before any financial aid.
Net Price
What your family actually pays after grants and scholarships. Varies by income — use each school's Net Price Calculator for your exact number.
Admit Rate
% of applicants offered admission. Lower = more selective. 🔴 Red = hardest (<15%), 🟠 Orange = selective (16–30%), 🟡 Amber = moderate (31–55%), 🟢 Green = accessible (>55%).
